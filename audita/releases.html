Audita
SPL meter & hearing-dose tracker for macOS
Version 1.2May 2026Latest
  • NewSend to Lyra… — after a target-level calibration, hand the position straight into a Lyra reference slot with one click. Audita writes the calibrated dB SPL via the lyra://reference/{n}/save URL, and the slot starts reading as e.g. "Mix — 85 dB SPL". Requires Lyra 1.4 or later.
  • ImprConservative mode — optional dose accumulation using max(dBA, dBC), for bass-heavy work.
  • FixNIOSH state-machine corrections for sub-minute exposures and rapid session start/stop.
  • FixMic-correction profiles now persist correctly across input-device changes.

  • NewMonitoring Discipline — a live on-target % chip in the menu bar with a green / yellow / orange ladder, tracking how consistently you monitor at your chosen reference.
  • NewMonitoring alerts — opt-in notifications when your level drifts or sustains a deviation off target.
  • NewShareable session reports — every session generates a one-page PDF with the SPL chart and stats grid.
  • ImprSession auto-detection now recognises 15 DAWs out of the box.
